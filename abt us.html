Our History

Bakers Bastion was founded in 2009 by 4 passionate bakers who came together because of their shared love for baking.
At first, they were just all students trying to get by school while baking in their free time, but their passion for baking motivated them to turn baking into something more than just a hobby in their free time.
They start small, just making cakes for their loved ones, but soon they expanded into a cake shop due to many praises and requests from other people and realising how a simple treat can bring everyoe together, the 4 of them come together and created bakers bastion after countless hours of hard work, trial and errors and perfecting their recipies eventually growing into a successful cake shop.
today, bakers bastion is a well known cake shop in the city, famous for its unique and delicious cake flavours made with dedication and care.
